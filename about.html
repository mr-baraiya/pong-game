History of Pong

Pong was one of the earliest arcade video games, created by Atari and released in 1972. It simulated table tennis (ping-pong) and became the first commercially successful video game, helping to establish the video game industry. The simple yet challenging gameplay has made Pong a lasting icon in gaming history.
