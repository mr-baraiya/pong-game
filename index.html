How to Play Pong Game

Playing Pong is simple yet addictive. Use the arrow keys or W/S keys to move your paddle up and down. Your goal is to score points by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle. The first player to reach 7 points wins!

  • Player 1 Controls: W (Up) and S (Down) or Arrow Keys
  • Player 2 Controls: Arrow Keys (in 2-Player mode)
  • Start/Pause: Spacebar or on-screen buttons
  • Mobile Support: Touch controls for smartphones and tablets
